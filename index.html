Chloe White.

One of Australia's most credentialled experts at the intersection of government policy, financial markets, and emerging technology.

Chloe was the formally appointed international regulatory policy advisor to Dubai's Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (VARA) during the formation of its licensing framework — the world's first regulatory regime of its kind. Before founding Riskmastery in 2023, she led the Australian Government's National Blockchain Roadmap, participated in the Australian Senate Inquiry on Fintech (producing its most-cited submission — 46 Senate citations), and was the Treasury's lead analyst on fintech and crypto-assets.

The Firm

Built for

Riskmastery is a boutique regulatory affairs firm built to serve fast-growing fintech and digital asset businesses establishing operations in international jurisdictions.

Our core offering spans jurisdiction selection, VASP licensing applications, compliance and risk advice, regulator relations, and tokenisation strategy. Every engagement is led personally by Chloe White — bringing direct government and regulatory architecture experience to each mandate.

We serve exchanges, asset managers, token issuers, and early-stage start-ups. Engagements begin from USD 2,000. Payments accepted in multiple currencies.

FAQ

Common questions.

Riskmastery is a boutique licensing and regulatory affairs firm specialising in international virtual asset regulation. We help fintech and digital asset businesses navigate the licensing process in select jurisdictions, manage regulator relationships, and build compliance frameworks that hold up to scrutiny.

Every engagement is led personally by Chloe White. We do not operate as a large consulting firm — we offer senior, expert-led advisory with a direct line to the person who helped architect the rules you're operating under.

We work with exchanges, brokerages, asset managers, token issuers, custody providers, and early-stage start-ups — at every stage of growth, from pre-incorporation through to offshore expansion.

Clients come from across the digital asset value chain: we have supported projects across every major market.

Riskmastery offers flexible engagement models — from one-off advisory calls and scoped project engagements through to longer-term retainer relationships. Service packages begin from USD 2,000, making high-quality expert advisory accessible to businesses at every stage, including early start-ups.
